Robust Robot Vision Evaluation: ResNet-18 vs SIFT–BoW–SVM (iCubWorld)

Computer Vision Reproducible Experiments Failure Analysis

Built two end-to-end recognition pipelines and evaluated robustness under viewpoint & illumination changes. Produced comparable metrics and documented trade-offs for engineering decisions.

  • Controlled sweeps: BoW vocab size, SVM C, KNN k, CNN lr/weight decay/batch size.
  • Reported confusion matrix and failure modes (shift sensitivity, class confusions).
  • Outcome: ResNet-18 ~98–99% vs classical pipeline ~56% (best run).
